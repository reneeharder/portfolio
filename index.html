About Me

Growing up my dad farmed our families small acerage of plums, peaches, and grapes. We learned early on the value of hard work, dedication, and perserverance.

After graduating from California State University Fresno, I spend the next 20 years working in local and global non-profits companies. While I loved the missions of those agencies, it was time to learn something new. Now, my focus is on creating beautiful websites for customers.

I live in Reedley, CA with my husband, 4 active kids, and one black lab that helps keep my sanity. I enjoy hiking, road trips, and being outside.
