About Me

Growing up I helped my dad farm our small family farm of plums, peaches, and grapes. I learned early on the value of hard work, dedication, and perserverance.

After graduating from California State University Fresno, I spent the next 20 years working in local and global non-profits companies. While I loved the missions of those agencies, it was time to learn something new. Now, my focus is on creating beautiful websites for customers.

I live in Reedley, CA with my husband, 4 active kids, and one black lab that helps keep my sanity. I enjoy hiking, road trips, and being outside.
